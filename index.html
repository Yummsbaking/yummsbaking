Our Story

Made at Home,
Served with Soul

Hi! I'm the baker behind Yummsbaking — a passionate home baker based in Naharlagun, Arunachal Pradesh who believes every bite should make you smile.

Every cake, cheesecake, and muffin is handcrafted in small batches using the finest local ingredients. No preservatives, no shortcuts — just real butter, fresh eggs, and love.

From classic birthday cakes to the iconic Basque Burnt Cheesecake — big bakery quality with the warmth of a home kitchen.
